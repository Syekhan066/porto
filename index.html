About Me

Introduction

I am a student of Manufacturing Engineering Technology/Technician at Universitas Negeri Malang, expected to graduate in August 2027. I have more than a year of experience as a Logistics Planner at PT. ASTRA DAIHATSU MOTOR, one of the leading automotive manufacturers in Indonesia. My core competencies include SAP ERP, SAP Products, and Logistic Support.

As a Logistics Planner, I was involved in various projects and committees related to plant administration, quality control, and business intelligence. I also had the opportunity to learn from the consumable warehouse operations, from receiving to issuing goods, transfer posting, and logistics support. Additionally, I completed a three-month internship at PT Indolakto, a dairy company, as a Support Analyst in the quality assurance division, where I conducted milk pH analysis and supported the quality testing process. Through these experiences, I developed valuable skills in data analysis, problem-solving, teamwork, and communication. I am motivated by learning new things, improving processes, and delivering results. I can bring diverse perspectives and experiences to the team and contribute to the organization's goals and culture.
